<span style="color:gray;">Shield</span>: 

Shield is a form of tanking, applying a buff to nearby allies that negates damage they take
for a certain amount of hits.

Pressing Prone (X) buff all nearby allies with the "shielded" buff, which negates all damage
taken for an amount of hits based on the tier of the caster.

Stamina Cost: 25%
Radius: 2400 (8 Foundations)
