<span style="color:orange;">Meltdown</span>: A very high potential, yet very dangerous ability. Meltdown creatures can
overheat themselves, sacrificing their own health, to deal stacking damage to enemies in
the immediate area.

Holding Prone(X) will force you into Meltdown mode. You will lose health in Meltdown
mode, and the rate at which health is lost will speed up the longer you are in Meltdown, but
the damage also increases the longer you are in Meltdown.

-----
Health Cost Per Second: 0.5% + 0.5% Per Second.
Damage Per Second: 50% of Ability Damage + 12.5% of Ability Damage Per Second.
Meltdown Radius: 1800 (6 Foundations).

Variant Information:
Spawn Chance: 01.28%

Other Information:
Cooldown 1: 60.0
Cooldown 2: 0.0
Uses Activated Ability When Dealing Damage: false
Uses Activated Ability When Taking Damage: false
