<span style="color:orange;">Detonate</span>: Detonate is a very dangerous ability, if you're able to kill your targets. Detonate is
a passive ability, which when hitting an enemy will debuff them for 5 seconds, indicated by
a head on fire. If the enemy is killed while debuffed, it will explode, dealing damage to
everything around it. There is also a chance that the debuffed enemy will explode when
dealing damage to the Detonate creature.

-----
Debuff Duration: 5s
Explosion Radius: 3000 (10 Foundations)
Explosion Damage: 100% of Detonate creature's Ability Damage
Chance for enemy to explode when dealing damage to Detonate Creature: 20%

Variant Information:
Spawn Chance: 00.85%

Other Information:
Cooldown 1: 0.0
Cooldown 2: 0.0
Uses Activated Ability When Dealing Damage: true
