<span style="color:green;">Tornado</span>: A terrifying force of Nature, Tornado creatures can spawn a powerful twister in
front of them, that can pick up, damage, and toss your enemies with great force.

Pressing Prone(X) creates a large Tornado in front of the Tornado Creature, that will pick up
creatures and hold them for a short period, dealing Wind damage and stunning them. If an
enemy dies inside of the Tornado, its corpse will be flung outward.
