<span style="color:yellow;">Loot</span>: Loot creatures are very elusive, and made of solid gold. They are very fast, have huge
damage resistance, and drop a cache of items when killed. They are the easest way to get
higher quality Ark Omega gear.

Loot dinos can only be tamed using Unique Kibble.
