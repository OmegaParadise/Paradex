<span style="color:blue;">Supernova</span>: A massive AOE damage dealer. During combat, they can release a powerful
Supernova blast that deals devistating damage to enemies and causes a very brief stun. Its
best to avoid a Supernova creature until you're ready.

Pressing Prone(X) causes a massive Supernova explosion, dealing significant damage to
anything in the area along with a short stun.
