<span style="color:blue;">Anti-Matter</span>: The 7th variant of Cosmic, Anti-Matter creatures' sole intended purpose is to
modify ability cooldowns.

Pressing Prone(X) creates a blast that zeroeos out the cooldown on ally abilities, while
triggering the cooldown timer on enemy abilities.

-----
Cooldown: 60s
Stamina Cost: 50%
Radius: 3000 (10 Foundations).
Applies a buff to allies that prevents Anti-Matter cooldown reset for 5 seconds.
Applies a buff to enemies that prevents Anti-Matter cooldown trigger for 45 seconds.
