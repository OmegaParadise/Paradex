<span style="color:purple;">Hive</span>: The 7th Variant of Summoner. Overseer summons a group of explosive bees,
that swarm around their enemy waiting to die. These bees glow with the color of the
summoner group before exploding and dealing aoe damage. If the bees are killed before
they explode, then their explosions deal reduced damage.

Pressing Prone(X) spawns a number of Martyr Bees, whos only purpose is to blow
themselves up and deal area damage to whatever they lock onto.
