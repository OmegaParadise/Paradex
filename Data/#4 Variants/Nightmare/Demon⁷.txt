<span style="color:black;">Demon</span>: The 7th Variant of Nightmare. Demon's have the ability to possess enemies, taking
control of them, causing damage to them, using them to attack other enemies or allies, and
more. While possessing another enemy, the demon cannot be damaged, and its location
marked by a black soul.

Pressing Prone(X) will possess the targeted enemy, taking control of it for a short duration.
During this time, you can use it to attack other enemies, or just sit idle with it allowing your
allies to attack it.
