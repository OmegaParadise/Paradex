<span style="color:red;">Psychosis</span>: A Psychosis creature has an ear-piercing screech that sounds all other nearby
creatures into a state of Psychosis, forcing them to attack eachother without prejudice. A
player's own mount will being attacking and ignoring the players control. This can also
prove valuable against enemies, as it will force them to attack other nearby creatures, even
their own minions.

Pressing Prone(X) releases an ear-shattering screech that drives enemies insane. They will
become hyper aggressive, attacking the closest thing to them.
