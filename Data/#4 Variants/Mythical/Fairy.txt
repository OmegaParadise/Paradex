<span style="color:lightgreen;">Fairy</span>: Natural healers. A fairy will make an invaluable ally in battle, as it will heal all nearby
allies for a % of their max health over time. An AOE healer that uses nothing more than its
own Stamina to heal.

Pressing Prone(X) will cause it to heal any nearby ally on your team for a % of their health
per second. Pressing X again will toggle it off and trigger the cooldown. The Fairy cannot
attack while healing.

-----
Cooldown: 60s
Stamina Drain Per Second: 5%
Healing Amount: (0.5% x Tier) of Max Health Per Second
Healing Radius: 3000 (10 Foundations)

Variant Information:
Spawn Chance: 02.13%

Other Information:
Cooldown 1: 30.0
Cooldown 2: 0.0
