<span style="color:lightgreen;">Phoenix</span>: Following the mythology surrounding the Phoenix, when killed it will simply rise
from the ashes to be born again. Phoenix creatures can rapidly restore their own health
when injured. When killed, their corpse will set ablaze, damaging enemies in the area. If its
corpse is not harvested within 10 seconds, the Phoenix will resurrect.

Pressing Prone(X) will rapidly restore a large portion of the Phoenix creatures health over
time.

-----
Cooldown: 30s
Stamina Cost: 50%
Health Restored: 10% per second for 5 seconds (50% of max overall)
Corpse Burn before Resurrection Duration: 10s
Corpse Burn AOE Damage: 1% of enemies max health per second.
Corpse Burn AOE Damage Radius: 1000 (3.33 foundations)
Phoenix Variants do not lose a tier when sent to the Reliquary.

Variant Information:
Spawn Chance: 01.7%

Other Information:
Cooldown 1: 30.0
Cooldown 2: 0.0
Uses Activated Ability When Dealing Damage: false
Uses Activated Ability When Taking Damage: true
